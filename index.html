HOW IT WORKS.

The game in 4 steps

  1. SPIN the wheel of nations.
  2. PICK one player from that nation. ★ NATURAL = your role's open slot. ↺ OUT OF POS = the natural slot is taken; you'll fill a nearby one and take a −1 OVR penalty.
  3. REPEAT until your 4-3-3 is full.
  4. SUBMIT your eleven to the leaderboard.

Resources

  • 5 SKIPS — pass on a nation without filling a slot. Limited.
  • 3 SWAPS — remove a placed player, unlock that nation, re-spin to refill.

Chemistry (FIFA-style)

Each player gets up to 3 chem dots based on how many XI teammates share their league. Every 6 total chem points = +1 OVR to your team rating.

Why is Italy / Denmark / Nigeria not in CLASSIC?

CLASSIC mode = the actual 48 teams that qualified for the 2026 World Cup. Those three didn't qualify. To play with them, switch to TOP 50 (premium) which includes elite missed nations.

What's the difference between CLASSIC, TOP 50, and LEGENDS?

CLASSIC: 48 World Cup qualified squads, official 26-man rosters where available. TOP 50 (premium): elite footballing nations — qualified and missed. LEGENDS (premium): all-time greats at peak career ratings (Pelé 99, Maradona 99, Cruyff 98).

How does the paywall work?

A single $4.99 one-time payment unlocks both TOP 50 and LEGENDS, forever. No subscription. Future premium modes are included free.

What happens if my natural-position slot is filled?

The player goes to another open slot in the same line (e.g., a CB into the RB slot) and takes a −1 OVR penalty. The slot border turns gold so you can see who's out of position.

Why does my XI take a −1 hit per OOP player?

Real football logic — a winger forced to play fullback isn't as effective. The penalty rewards roster discipline.

Can I share my XI?

Submit your eleven with your name. It appears on the public leaderboard. Share-as-image is coming soon.
