Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Perfect Eleven (“we,” “us,” the “Game”) is a free browser game available at perfect-eleven.vercel.app. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the Game, how it is used, and the choices you have. We have tried to keep both the Game and this policy simple: there are no accounts, and we collect as little as possible.

Information you choose to share

The Game does not require you to register or sign in. The only personal information we receive is what you voluntarily enter when you submit a lineup to the public leaderboard: a display name and an optional city. This information is shown publicly on the leaderboard alongside your team. Please do not enter real personal details you do not wish to be public — a nickname is fine.

Information stored on your device

The Game saves your progress, settings, streaks and any premium unlock locally in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device, is not transmitted to us as an account, and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Cookies and advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertising, which helps keep the Game free. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of some third-party vendors’ use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices.

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and other regions where consent is required, a consent message is shown and advertising behaviour reflects your choices.

Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage of the Game (for example, how many people visit and which features are used). This data is not used to identify individuals.

Payments

Optional premium modes are sold through Stripe. If you make a purchase, your payment is processed entirely by Stripe; we do not receive or store your full card details. Stripe’s handling of your data is governed by its own privacy policy.

Children

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • Clear your local data any time via your browser settings.
  • Manage or opt out of personalised ads via the links above.
  • Use the Game without submitting to the leaderboard if you prefer to share nothing publicly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “last updated” date above.
